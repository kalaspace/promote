# 04 — External Views (critics, journalists, ex-colleagues, court findings)

**Scope**: what OTHERS say about Oussama Ammar. This is the sensitive dimension — the Skill must capture controversy honestly without libel or whitewashing. Court-established facts and top-tier journalism only.

## Court-established facts (adjudicated)

### Cayman Islands — Grand Court judgment, Dec 5–6, 2023
- **Charges**: abus de confiance (breach of trust) + faux et usage de faux (forgery & use of forged documents)
- **Verdict**: liable
- **Damages**: €7.3M total — €3.15M restitution + €3.95M in lost earnings
- **Parties**: Ammar + his entities Fabuleo Ltd and Aletheis The First Ltd
- **Sources**: PROCES.FR court database, Tech.eu (Dec 2023)

### UK High Court judgment, Mar/Apr 2025
- **Charges**: fraud + breach of fiduciary duty
- **Verdict**: liable (defense was struck for non-compliance with court orders — he did not meaningfully defend)
- **Damages**: £6.48M (≈€7.56M)
- **Status**: non-appealable as of Jan 2026
- **Sources**: Sifted (exclusive), Maddyness

### France — Nanterre Criminal Court, Jun 2018 (earlier conviction, often overlooked)
- **Context**: Ammar as CEO of Be Sport (2011)
- **Charges**: falsifying invoices, unauthorized cash withdrawals — €5K false invoice to LeWeb sponsor, withdrawals without documentation
- **Verdict**: guilty — 4 months imprisonment, suspended
- **Source**: Entreprisma, Wikipedia (fr)

### France — Ongoing proceedings (2023–2026)
- **Authority**: Parquet National Financier (PNF) — National Financial Prosecutor's Office
- **Charges under investigation**: tax fraud + money laundering related to tax fraud
- **Trigger**: Ministry of Economy complaint, June 2023; PNF preliminary investigation formally opened Jan 2026 per Maddyness
- **Feb 26, 2025**: Ammar taken into custody at Nice airport upon return from Dubai. Interrogated ~4 hours by BRDA (Brigade for the Repression of Clever Crime — Brigade de Répression de la Délinquance Astucieuse). Released same day. Not formally charged as of writing.
- **Sources**: Maddyness, Nice Presse, Sifted

## The core embezzlement allegations (Family case)

Detailed breakdown per Sifted investigation (2022) + TechCrunch (Mar 2022):

**Time period**: 2019–2021

**Mechanism**: Ammar was given ~€3M to manage investment vehicles on behalf of ~100 community investors:
- **€884,055** from 56 investors — Airbnb secondary shares
- **€804,100** from 49 investors — SpaceX secondary shares
- **€1,043,932** from 98 investors — Stripe secondary shares
- Additional: ~€3.9M raised Oct 2019–Mar 2020 for Algolia secondaries, only partially invested

**Alleged use**: diverted to personal accounts + Domaine d'Ablon (Normandy luxury estate / Petit Manoir construction)

**Zagury (co-founder) characterization to Sifted (2022)**: *"He pretended that he had these deals and he raised all of this money. And then he used it to buy himself a castle."*

**Criminal complaint filed**: Mar 22, 2022 (Paris) — charges: breach of trust, forgery, use of forgery — damages claimed €3M+
