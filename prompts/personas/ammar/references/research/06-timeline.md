## Narrative summary

### Early life (1986–2008)
Born Beirut. Franco-Lebanese family. Father absent. Mother worked as housekeeper. Moved through Kinshasa to Tours, France. Philosophy studies at Sorbonne + EHESS — this becomes central to his identity ("philosopher-turned-entrepreneur"). Claims early wins (hedge fund work, first company sold at 19) that are part of his narrative but hard to verify independently.

### Pre-Family ventures (2008–2012)
**Hypios** (2008–2011): co-founded, went bankrupt, audit flagged ~€200K in personal expenses and self-dealing — **a pre-figuration of the later pattern**.

**Be Sport** (2011, 6 months as CEO): €150K allegedly embezzled; €5K forged invoice. Complaint filed Dec 2011. Converted to criminal conviction in 2018.

These two episodes — one bankrupt with financial controversy, one resulting in a forgery conviction — were largely forgotten or overlooked during his rise.

### The Family's rise (2013–2017)
**2013**: co-founded with Zagury and Colin in Paris. Seed from Index Ventures.
**2014**: Koudetat, Les Barbares Attaquent, Welcome to The Family publication. Ammar emerges as the movement's public voice.
**2015**: Meets Macron. London office.
**2016–2017**: Intellectual shift from Silicon Valley optimism toward power/leverage/complexity; "barbarians" framing hardens.

Real investments, real portfolio companies, real strategic insight. The first five years are coherent.

### Hidden decline (2018–2021)
**2018**: Nanterre conviction (4 months suspended) — surprisingly little reputational damage. Meanwhile The Family raises €25M+ across SPVs.

**2019–2021**: Alleged diversion of €3–4.5M labeled for unicorn secondaries to personal use and the Petit Manoir construction. 39 transfers (~€803K) from Family to personal. Investor disclosure campaigns intensify. Co-founders distance.

**Sep/Nov 2021**: forced out; publicly framed as voluntary Web3 pivot.

### Legal collapse (2022–2025)
**Mar 2022**: criminal complaint + civil lawsuits filed. Media storm.
**Dec 2023**: Cayman judgment — €7.3M.
**Feb 2025**: Nice airport custody.
**Mar/Apr 2025**: UK judgment — £6.48M.
**Jun 2023 → Jan 2026**: PNF investigation (tax fraud + money laundering).

Three jurisdictions; consistent findings.

### Post-Family era (2022–2026)
**Dubai** base. **Labyrinth** accelerator announced. Substack, Twitter, LinkedIn, seminars continue. "My Truth" (Nov 2025) reframes everything as political/philosophical conflict. Court-ordered demolition of the Petit Manoir becomes content spectacle. New complaint (harassment) Feb 2026. No settlements. No apologies. No charges yet formally filed in France despite 3+ years of investigation.
