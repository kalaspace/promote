## Source material distilled

- Eugene Schwartz, *Breakthrough Advertising* (Boardroom Books, 1966; reprinted by Bottom Line, 2017). The canonical text. Out-of-print most years; the 2017 reprint is hard-cover and was priced at ~$125. Earlier editions trade for $700-1500 in collectors markets.
- Gary Halbert, *The Boron Letters* (1984-86, compiled). Halbert was a Schwartz disciple; his letters preserve and extend the principles.
- Modern decompositions and teaching:
  - Stefan Georgi (Copy Accelerator) — applies Schwartz's awareness stages to modern direct response.
  - Justin Goff — newsletter / training programs that explicitly recite Schwartz's frameworks.
  - Kim Krause Schwalm — A-list copywriter who teaches Schwartz pedagogically.
- Citations from Hormozi, Brunson, Welsh — they all use Schwartz's awareness ladder; rarely credit him by name.
