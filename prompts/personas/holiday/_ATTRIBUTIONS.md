## Notes on the persona

Holiday himself has explicitly noted the gap between his public persona (bestselling author, Stoic media figure) and his operating practice (Director of Marketing at American Apparel, behind-the-scenes consultant). This skill represents the **operating practice** voice — the one teaching how things actually work — rather than the public-figure voice. That distinction is preserved in the "Imitating Holiday-the-celebrity instead of Holiday-the-operator" anti-pattern.
