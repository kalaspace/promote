## Scope (what this skill covers)

This skill captures Brett Curry's **Amazon Ads + YouTube Ads cross-channel DTC playbook**. The Curry uniqueness: most operators specialize in ONE platform (Amazon-only, or YouTube-only, or Meta-only). Curry combines Amazon AND YouTube uniquely well for DTC because OMG Commerce was originally a Google Premier Partner agency that expanded into Amazon — they understand both attribution worlds and the halo between them.

- **Amazon Sponsored Ads foundation**: Sponsored Products (branded defense / category / auto harvest), Sponsored Brands + Sponsored Brands Video, Sponsored Display (PDP retargeting + competitor-ASIN). ACoS calibration to contribution margin.
- **Amazon DSP gating + scaling**: 4-gate readiness check (PPC plateau + $25K/mo PPC + 7,500 sessions/mo + reviews ≥4.0), 1:3-1:4 PPC:DSP ratio, 33/33/33 funnel split (bottom remarketing / mid competitor + in-market / top in-market broad + streaming TV), 14-day last-touch attribution honesty.
- **ACoS vs TACoS discipline**: ACoS daily (tactical) / TACoS weekly (strategic), brand cannibalization diagnosis (5-15% target on branded defense), TACoS targets by stage (Launch 25-40% / Growth 15-25% / Mature 8-15%).
- **YouTube three-layer funnel stack**: Cold (Demand Gen + Video Reach, tCPM, broad in-market + custom intent + lookalikes), Warm (Demand Gen, tCPA, site visitors + engaged viewers + email match), Hot (Video Action + Demand Gen retargeting, tROAS, cart abandoners + PDP viewers). Run simultaneously, never sequentially.
- **The 5-second hook + 30-90s script anatomy**: 5 hook categories (pattern interrupt / audience call-out / contrarian claim / visual demo / VOC cold open), 6-beat script anatomy (hook → problem → reveal+demo → proof → offer → CTA).
- **Pre-roll vs in-stream skippable strategy**: skippable Video Action / Demand Gen as workhorse (CPV, 5-second skip gate, ≥30s = strong intent), bumpers/non-skippable as brand-lift only.
- **Demand Gen (Google's 2024 product)**: warm + hot layer, vertical + horizontal creative, lookalike-from-converters > lookalike-from-page-viewers.
- **Cross-channel synergy plays**: YouTube → Amazon halo (branded search lift drives Amazon organic + branded PPC), YouTube → Meta retargeting (YouTube-warmed Custom Audiences cut Meta CPMs 2-3x), Amazon DSP + YouTube top-funnel complementarity, Meta → Amazon flywheel.
- **Halo measurement KPIs**: MER (the headline number), branded search lift (Search Console + Trends), Amazon TACoS trend during YouTube ramp, new customer % of revenue. The 90-day cross-channel scaling rule: judge MER at month 5-6, not channel-native ROAS at month 1.
- **Voice DNA**: DTC-pragmatic, "let me show you a client" case-study cadence, numbers-first, balanced (not hype-coach, not skeptic), Google Premier Partner authority.
