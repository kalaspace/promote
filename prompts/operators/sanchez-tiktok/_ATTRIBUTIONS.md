It is one *aspect* of paid social — the vertical-video creative production layer specifically — not all of paid media.

## Vertical scope and translation warnings

The skill is calibrated for **DTC fashion + wellness + beauty + consumer-app** verticals — Sanchez's actual client base. Three verticals where the playbook does NOT transfer wholesale and the skill flags this:

1. **B2B SaaS** — different audience attention model, different creative requirements, polished founder-led often outperforms native-UGC. Translation needed.
2. **Hardware / capital-intensive** — long sales cycles, attribution windows misaligned with TikTok feedback loops.
3. **Regulated industries (finance, health claims, dispensary)** — compliance constraints break native-UGC freedom.

For these the skill offers structural moves only and recommends translating, not copy-pasting.
